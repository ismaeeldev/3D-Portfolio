5. AI STYLE PARAMETERS & POST-PROCESSING GUIDE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To make these assets production-ready for your React site:

1. ASPECT RATIO (For Midjourney):
   Always add the `--ar 1:1` parameter to get a perfect square image, which is 
   crucial for matching logo and favicon containers.
   
2. BACKGROUND REMOVAL (Post-Processing):
   AI models generate backgrounds. Once you generate the logo:
   - Use a background remover (like remove.bg, Photoshop, or Photopea) to make 
     the background transparent.
   - For web integration, save the logo as a transparent `.png` or convert it 
     to `.svg` (using tools like Vectorizer.ai) for infinitely scalable vector code!

3. MATCHING NAV & HERO SCALING:
   - Your site's header logo container uses a `w-9 h-9` ratio (36px). Ensure the 
     cropped logo has thin transparent margins around it so it displays large 
     and clear inside the circular navbar frame.
   - For the favicon, convert the transparent PNG to a `.ico` file or a 32x32 PNG, 
     saving it into `/public/ico.ico`.
